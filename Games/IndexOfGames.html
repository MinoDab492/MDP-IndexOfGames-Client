Controls

Snake

Arrow keys to move, simply avoid hitting yourself and get the apple. If you collide with the wall, you will come out on the other side.

Get Out

Use the arrow keys to move the platform, and space to spawn a ball. When the ball collides with a tile on top, the tile breaks. Try to break all of them with as few balls as possible.

Flappy JPEG

Enter to start, and restart, space to jump. Same as flappy bird really, just incredibly scuffed.

Pong

Two Player Game! Player One, use W to move up, and S to move down. Player 2, use up and down arrow keys. Same as normal pong.

Scufftris (Tetris)

Left and right arrow keys to move the tiles over, and up arrow key to rotate.
